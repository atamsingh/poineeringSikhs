Celebrating the achievements of Pioneer Sikhs of Ontario

Pape Gurdwara, Toronto

Awarded for

Establishing Community Roots in Ontario

Sharomni Sikh Sangat Toronto (known as Pape Gurdwara for its location on pape Avenue, Toronto) is the first Gurdwara to be established in Ontario in 1969. The property was purchased by Jimmyat Singh Gill, Mohinder Singh and Krishanpal Singh Sud, intrust.

Ishar Singh

Awarded for

Recognition of Sikh Articles of Faith in Ontario

In 1975, Ishar Singh was denied employment due to his turban and beard. Mr. Singh took matters to the Ontario Human Rights Tribunal and won. This was the first case of its kind for Sikhs in Ontario.

Pingalwara Society of Ontario

Awarded for

Sewa (Selfless Service) in Ontario

Pingalwara Society of Ontario established in 1987 by Abnash K. Kang, Amarjit K. Dhaliwal and Lakhwinder K. pannu who began their philanthorpic journey by collecting donations for the Pingalwara Charitable Society, founded by Bhagat Puran Singh.
